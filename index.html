Crystal Game

Game Rules

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on the crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The values of each crystal will be hidden from you, track your total score as you click each crystal.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
